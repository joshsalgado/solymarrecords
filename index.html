About

Solymar Records is a Los Angeles-based independent label that releases minimal/deep house and techno influenced by Latin American roots. The imprint’s latest release Solymar Records Vol. 1 features Argentinian vocalist Ema Yelpez and Uruguayan vocalist Paz delivering hypnotic vocals in Spanish and Portuguese over latin percussion infused Minimal/Deep Tech production by Salgado and Valentin Thomas. Previous releases include Salgado - Jajaja, a dub influenced Minimal/Deep Tech track featuring AI generated vocals, and Salgado, Valentin Thomas - Miami, a deep house track including latin percussion and tropical sounds. Submit demos at solymarrecords@gmail.com.
